Our App

Distinct Roles
Our mobile and web apps use the roles teacher and dancer, having in mind that they are a counterpart of each other, complementing themselves in a constant, attentive and precise tracking of physical activity. Ech role has a differente functionalities.

Professor
The professor app contains two simple functionalities, taking attentance and verification of injuries, this is filtered by the grade and group the student is in. This was created with the intention of avoiding loss of essential time.

Dancer
The dancer app comprises the most complex range of software technologies within our collection, as it focuses on our main customers. It contains dynamic interfaces supplied with information obtained from our database connected to the smart pointe sensors. It's main functions are the addition of schedules, display of warnings according to the risk of injuries detected and daily and general performance reports on the following areas:

  • High Impact
  • Turns
  • Brush
  • Balance
  • Legs Height
  • Warnings
