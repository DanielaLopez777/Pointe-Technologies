Hiring Plans

Academic
The academic recruitment plan is suitable for high performance classical dance teaching institutions with vocational degrees. This plan includes a number of licenses for the dancer application along with its sensor kit and qr-coded fitting and usage instructions. Also, a number of licenses for the teacher application.

Professional
The professional hiring plan is suitable for classical dancers who do not work in a fixed ballet company, or work in one that does not have this resource and want to acquire it for their personal training. This plan includes the license for the dancer application and its sensor kit with its respective instructions for use and placement in qr code.
