About us

Pointé is a collection of technological products focused on the classical area of dance, serving as monitoring instruments to improve the dancer's personal and academic training. Pointé represents the perfect fusion between classic and modern, uniting two completely different centuries and disciplines in a complementary way. Pointé is the place where the classical and innovation come together as one.

Objective

In an art as conservative and demanding as ballet, dancers tend to face multiple challenges, both physical and mental. One of the most common physical challenges that tend to slow down an artistic career in its prime is injury. The purpose of this innovative range of interconnected technologies is to reduce them by monitoring movements, simulating some of the health and training functions of a smartwatch, but in a specialized way to this discipline through a mobile application.

Vision

Pointé is expected to be a leader in the market, becoming a vital tool for the academic and professional training of a classical dancer in important companies and academies within the European Union such as the Bolshoi, Paris Opera Ballet, Mariinsky Ballet, Royal Ballet Academy, Royal Opera House, or La Scala in Milan.

Mission

To provide support to students, teachers and professionals of classical dance throughout their careers. Pointé seeks to take care of the physical performance of the dancer in order to enhance the results, and in turn, reduce the effort or overload in certain parts of the body that could cause a severe injury that could represent at some point the end of a career.
